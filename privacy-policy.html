Gymco Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Gymco is a fitness and nutrition tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

Gymco may collect or store the following information when you use the app:

  • Account information such as email address and sign-in details.
  • Profile information such as name, fitness goals, height, weight, and preferences.
  • Workout data such as training days, exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout history.
  • Nutrition data such as meals, calories, macros, fiber, notes, and meal templates.
  • Progress data such as body measurements, progress notes, and progress photos if you choose to add them.
  • Custom exercise images or meal images if you choose to upload or save them.
  • App settings such as language, theme, and saved preferences.

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Create and manage your Gymco account.
  • Save your workouts, meals, progress, and preferences.
  • Sync your data across sessions and supported devices.
  • Improve app functionality, reliability, and user experience.
  • Provide features such as workout planning, nutrition tracking, and progress tracking.

Data Storage and Sync

Gymco stores some data locally on your device so the app can work smoothly. If you create or sign in to an account, your app data may also be synced to Mortarbase/Supabase services so it can be restored and kept available for your account.

Photos and Media

Gymco may request access to your camera or photo library only when you choose to add a meal photo, progress photo, or custom exercise image. These images are used for the features you select.

Health and Fitness Notice

Gymco provides fitness and nutrition tracking tools. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making major changes to your exercise, nutrition, or health routine.

Third-Party Services

Gymco may use third-party services such as Mortarbase/Supabase for authentication, database storage, and file storage. These providers may process data according to their own privacy and security practices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is used to operate Gymco and provide the app features. Data may be shared only when needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect users, or maintain security.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account from inside the app settings. Deleting your account removes account data stored by Gymco for that user, subject to any legal or security retention requirements.

Children

Gymco is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and remove it when appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
