Delete Your Gymco Account

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Gymco lets users request deletion of their account and associated app data. You can delete your account directly inside the Gymco app or contact us for help.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open the Gymco app.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, email us at iamkhaledhajjo@gmail.com using the email address connected to your Gymco account. Please include "Gymco account deletion request" in the subject line.

Data Deleted

When your Gymco account is deleted, we delete account-associated app data such as:

  • Profile data and preferences.
  • Workout plans, workout logs, sets, reps, and weights.
  • Meal logs, meal templates, nutrition values, and notes.
  • Progress photos and custom exercise or meal images saved for your account.
  • Custom exercises and app settings associated with your account.

Data Retention

Account deletion is intended to remove your account-associated Gymco data. Some limited records may be retained temporarily where required for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, backup recovery, or technical operations. Backup copies are removed according to the normal backup lifecycle.
